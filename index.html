How It Works

Simple workflow for both teams

1. Admin registers users

Sales executives, managers, and operations users are created in the admin portal with role-based access.

2. Users register leads

Sales team members capture company, contact, source, value, and next action from the user portal.

3. Progress is tracked by stage

Every lead moves through a pipeline like New Lead, Qualified, Proposal Sent, Negotiation, Won, or Lost.

4. Admin monitors quality and ownership

Managers review assignments, update probabilities, watch follow-ups, and keep the CRM process consistent.

Deployment Path

How to roll the CRM out across your organization

Pilot with one team first

Start with one sales team, validate fields and stages, then expand after the process is stable.

Move demo storage to a central database

Replace browser storage with PostgreSQL, SQL Server, or MySQL so the full company sees the same data.

Connect login to company identity

Use Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, or your internal SSO to secure both portals and control permissions.

Train, monitor, and govern

Standardize lead stages, assign ownership rules, and review adoption weekly after launch.
